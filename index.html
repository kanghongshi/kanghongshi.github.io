Kanghong Shi

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Australian Centre for Field Robotics (ACFR) at the University of Sydney. My PhD in control theory was completed at the CIICADA Lab at the Australian National University (ANU). My research interests include nonlinear systems, networked systems, switched systems, and learning-based control. Explore my projects and publications.
